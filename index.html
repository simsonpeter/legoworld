How to Play

  • Create your character using the "Your Character" option
  • Customize your minifigure with colors and expressions
  • Click on the world to place your character
  • Launch to space to see your character transform into an astronaut!
  • Return to Earth to switch back to your regular minifigure

Tip: Your character will now appear on the map exactly where you click!
